MSc Project · In Progress

SAV-SLAM: Semantic Visual-Audio SLAM

Exploring how auditory perception can augment visual SLAM systems. The project integrates audio foundation models alongside SAM (Segment Anything Model) into a SLAM baseline to enable multimodal scene understanding — moving beyond purely visual representations toward richer, hearing-aware robot perception.

SLAM SAM Audio Foundation Models Multimodal Perception PyTorch Robotics

CNN Typhoon Prediction

ML / CV Bachelor Project

Tropical cyclone intensity estimation from satellite imagery. Compares a baseline CNN against a CBAM-enhanced model with channel and spatial attention, trained on 5,573 grayscale images with stratified 5-fold cross-validation. Achieved ~10 knots MAE.
